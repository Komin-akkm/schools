Perfect English Language Center

Location

Perfect English Language Center is located in No.81, Yadanapon 3rd Street, Hintha Township, Ayeyarwady Region, Myanmar. Being in Kanaung Su Quarter, near Hinthada University, the school is in a perfect location near Main Roads, markets and other facilities.

Services

We are offering the best education services in the local area to learners aged from 5 years up to adults with the most current and up-to-date materials, teaching aids and methodologies.We also provide training courses for those who are preparing for international exams and who want to study or work abroad. The teachers working here are high-qualified and well-efficient with over 15 years of teaching experience in English Language Teaching and Assessment.
